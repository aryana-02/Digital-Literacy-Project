# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before sharing information
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Never share OTPs with anyone

## UPI Safety Tips
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
- Do not approve unknown payment requests

## General Safety
- Keep your software and apps updated
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline 1930 in case of fraud
