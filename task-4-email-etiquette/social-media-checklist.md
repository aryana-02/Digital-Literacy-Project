# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use respectful language online
- Share useful and positive content
- Maintain privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing
- Be professional in public posts

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying
- Avoid spreading fake news
- Do not overshare private details
